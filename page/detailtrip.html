About the Trip

Experience the magical sunrise at Mount Bromo, one of Indonesia's most iconic volcanoes. This midnight adventure takes you through the Sea of Sand in a 4x4 Jeep, hiking up to the crater rim to witness the earth breathing smoke and sulfur.

Perfect for solo travelers, couples, and small groups looking for a hassle-free adventure. We handle all the logistics so you can focus on the breathtaking views.

Itinerary

02:00 AM

Pick up at Meeting Point

Gather at the designated meeting point in Malang or Probolinggo. Briefing by the tour leader.

03:30 AM

Arrive at Sunrise Viewpoint

Jeep ride to Penanjakan or King Kong Hill. Coffee break while waiting for sunrise.

05:00 AM

Golden Sunrise Moment

Enjoy the spectacular sunrise view over Mount Bromo, Batok, and Semeru.

07:00 AM

Bromo Crater Hike

Descend to the Sea of Sand and hike up to the crater rim (approx 30 mins walk).
